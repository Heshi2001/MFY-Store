Refund & Cancellation Policy

We aim to provide a smooth shopping experience. Read our refund and cancellation process below.

1. Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours if not yet shipped.

2. Refunds

Refunds are approved for defective or damaged items after review. It may take up to 7 business days for the refund to reflect.

3. Non-refundable Items

Personalized or clearance items are non-refundable unless they arrive damaged.
